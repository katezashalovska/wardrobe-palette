Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

At AI Wardrobe, we prioritize your trust and privacy. This Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

1. What Information Do We Collect?

We only collect data that is essential for the service to function:

  • Clothing Photos: Only the images you upload for AI analysis.
  • Image Metadata: To automatically detect categories and colors.
  • Device Information: Basic technical info to ensure app stability.

2. How Do We Use Your Data?

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • Creating your digital wardrobe.
  • Generating personalized style recommendations.
  • Improving our AI algorithms for clothing recognition.

3. Do We Share Data with Third Parties?

We do not sell your data. We may use cloud services (such as Google Cloud or AWS) for image processing and data storage, ensuring the highest level of security.

4. Your Rights

You have full control over your data. You can delete uploaded photos at any time or contact us to request the full deletion of your account and all associated data.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding our privacy policy, please contact us through the support section in the app or via email.
